How Compensation Voting Works

Vote on the compensation amount and payment structure for elected Multi-Sig signers. Your choices shape how the community rewards signers for their service.

  • Selection is per term (not monthly)
  • Median of all voters' median choices becomes the compensation
  • $200 minimum (symbolic, covers gas costs)
  • $3,000 maximum (meaningful but not excessive)
  • Adjustable in future elections
